Pizza Margherita

Pizza Margherita

Classic Italian pizza with fresh basil, mozzarella, and tomato sauce.

Prep Time: 30 min

Servings: 2-3

1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
3. Spread tomato sauce evenly.
4. Add slices of fresh mozzarella and basil leaves.
5. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt.
6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden.
7. Slice and enjoy warm.
Sushi

Sushi

Delicate rolls with fresh fish, rice, and nori seaweed.

Prep Time: 45 min

Servings: 2

1. Rinse sushi rice until water runs clear.
2. Cook rice and season with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
3. Place nori on bamboo mat, spread rice thinly.
4. Add fish, avocado, cucumber.
5. Roll tightly and slice with wet knife.
6. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
Burger

Burger

Juicy beef patty with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and special sauce.

Prep Time: 25 min

Servings: 1

1. Season beef patty with salt and pepper.
2. Grill over medium-high heat for 4-5 minutes per side.
3. Toast buns lightly.
4. Assemble burger with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and sauce.
5. Serve with a side of fries or salad.
